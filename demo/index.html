Website creation and management made easy

The Need for a Solution

For some time, the web development teams at MerchEngines have been in need of a true CMS (Content Management System) for the build-out and maintenance of their Landing Pages and Microsites. Many ideas have been discussed over the years ranging from Wordpress, to licensed 3rd party vendor solutions.

Experience working on the frontlines of the fulfillment department provided insight to what is required in a CMS for our teams to work at their optimal efficiency.

  • A lightweight system which is simple and intuitive
  • Modularity, the ability to easily add plugins and templates
  • Quick and easy setup (From start to finish)

The CMS Problem

Currently, landing page or microsite content management solutions (CMS) involve using large scale CMS' and blogging platforms.

While other Content Management Systems may serve the basic functions to create a Landing Page or Microsite, they fall behind sooner or later:

  • Systems like Drupal and Wordpress carry a lot of overhead
  • Long and tedious setup process, generally requiring SQL setup

A true, lean Content Management Solution is needed.

The Idea

A system was developed to create JSON files on the fly to store website data.

The advantage:

  1. Very easy system implementation
  2. No database setup
  3. Lightning-fast load time

By changing how content and data is stored on the file-system, files serve MUCH faster than the alternative. In addition, the lack of SQL use alleviates servers of unneccary strain.

Meet Shibui

Our use of ShibuiCMS

The implementation of ShibuiCMS within Hostopia/Deluxe systems would require little effort.

  • Create additional service which includes ShibuiCMS files and SSL
  • Use Shibui additional service whenever a landing page is needed.

ShibuiCMS's administration login is based off a master-domain. Any current Deluxe/Hostopia DB (IE MerchEngines) could act as the master-domain. Any account registered as an Admin for the related DB will have access to any Deluxe landing page. This results in extremely nimble administration creation.
